MonkeyMinds Video

Overlay Mode

Play/pause video with custom overlay control

Basic Overlay

Click overlay to play, click video to pause.

Click to Play

<div mm-tool="video" mm-video-mode="overlay">
  <video mm-video-element="player" src="video.mp4"></video>
  <div mm-video-element="overlay">
    <!-- Your overlay content -->
  </div>
</div>

Looping Video

Video loops automatically when it ends.

Looping Video

Click to start

<div mm-tool="video" 
     mm-video-mode="overlay"
     mm-video-loop="true">
  <!-- video + overlay -->
</div>

Muted Autoplay

Automatically starts playing (muted).

<div mm-tool="video" 
     mm-video-mode="overlay"
     mm-video-autoplay="true"
     mm-video-muted="true"
     mm-video-loop="true">
  <!-- video + overlay -->
</div>

Instant Transition

Overlay appears/disappears instantly (no fade).

No Fade Effect

<div mm-tool="video" 
     mm-video-transition="none">
  <!-- video + overlay -->
</div>

Custom Thumbnail Overlay

Use an image as the overlay.

<div mm-video-element="overlay">
  <img src="thumbnail.jpg" alt="Thumbnail">
  <!-- Play button overlay -->
</div>
